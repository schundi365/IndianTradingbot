   "atr_multiplier": 2.5

   ------------------------------------------
   "symbol_atr_multipliers": {
       "XAGUSD": 3.0,
       "GBPJPY": 2.5,
       "EURJPY": 2.5

   Add any symbol that needs wider/tighter stops

   --------------------------
   "trailing_tp_ratio": 0.5
   
   Example: Trail more aggressively
   "trailing_tp_ratio": 0.3
   
   Lower = trails closer to price (more aggressive)
   Higher = trails further from price (more conservative)

6. DISABLE TRAILING TP:
   ---------------------
   "enable_trailing_tp": false
