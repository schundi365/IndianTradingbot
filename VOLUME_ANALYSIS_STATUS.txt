HOW IT WORKS
================================================================================

TRADE FLOW:
1. Bot detects entry signal (MA crossover, RSI, MACD)
2. VOLUME ANALYSIS checks:
   - Is volume > 1.2x average? ✓
   - Is volume trend increasing? ✓
   - Does OBV confirm signal? ✓
   - Any divergence? ✓
3. If confirmed → Apply confidence boost (+5% to +15%)
4. If rejected → Skip trade
5. Adaptive risk uses updated confidence
6. Place trade with optimized parameters

CONFIDENCE BOOST:
- Above average volume: +5%
- Increasing volume trend: +5%
- OBV confirms signal: +5%
- Bullish/Bearish divergence: +10%
- Maximum boost: +15%
