╔══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╗
║                          WHY THIS WORKS                                      ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════╝

FUNDAMENTAL PRINCIPLE:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Trade WITH momentum, not against it!

RSI ZONES EXPLAINED:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

0-30:  OVERSOLD     = Price may bounce (don't sell here!)
30-50: BEARISH      = Good momentum for SELL trades ✅
50:    NEUTRAL      = Dividing line
50-70: BULLISH      = Good momentum for BUY trades ✅
70-100: OVERBOUGHT  = Price may reverse (don't buy here!)

WHY 50 IS THE KEY:
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RSI > 50 = Bulls in control (price rising faster than falling)
RSI < 50 = Bears in control (price falling faster than rising)

So:
BUY when RSI > 50  = Trading with the bulls ✅
SELL when RSI < 50 = Trading with the bears ✅
